Do YOU Need Help Decorating Your Home?

By Maddie

It’s 1937, does your house still look like your grandparents? Here are five EASY steps to making your house more modern. One, if your walls have bold colors or patterned wallpaper, switch them out for simple pale greens or blues with accents of tan or plum. Two, if you plan on renovation or re-building your house, use cement floors to reduce the need for load-bearing walls, introducing open-concept living with fewer walls and large windows. Three, use more practical furniture items such as couches, end tables and rugs to decorate your living or sitting room. Four, replace ornate banisters with clean-lined, almost blocky ones that feature storage for books and such. Five, pick a wall in a dining room for example and paint it a more vibrant color, like cobalt blue, while leaving the rest of the walls a pale almond or white. By following these five simple steps your house will be the talk of the neighborhood in no time.
