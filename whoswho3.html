Who is Who: George Milton

By Alice

Who is he?
After interviewing the known culprit of Lennie Smalls murder, George Milton, I learned key information from his background life and his relationship with Lennie. George is now thirty years old and was born in Auburn, California. At the age of thirteen, George's father left for New York so George went to live with Lennie because of their close family relationship. He claims that him and Lennie were so close they could be considered family. According to George, him and Lennie had a job in Weed until an incident terminated their time there which caused them to come to Tyler Ranch looking for a job. They had found a low paying job at the ranch and stayed there for three days until the murder occurred.
How was he involved in the case?
George is involved because he was convicted of the murder. George’s opinion on Lennie no longer being in his life is that he feels conflicted. He is sad for losing him but believes it was the right thing to do in the situation.
