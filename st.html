Slim’s Testimony

Today was Slim Johnson’s testimony. He was asked a series of questions in court about the killing of Lennie Small and his relationship between George Milton and Lennie Small. Slim stated
that he knew Lennie and George for a few days while they were on the ranch. Slim said that he saw George and Lennie as more than just friends on the ranch, they were like family. Prior to George killing Lennie, Slim left the group of guys and went to look for Lennie who he hadn't seen. Slim told the Judge that if George didn’t killed Lennie, Curley had a bad punishment in mind for Lennie after he found out that he killed his wife.
