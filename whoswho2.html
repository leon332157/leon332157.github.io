Who's Who: Chuck Long

By Emma

Who is he?
Chuck Long is 32 years old and is from Unionville, Nevada. He works on a goldmine in Ely, NV. Chuck was friends with Lennie and George. He met them on a highway in Ely. They traveled together for a few months until George and Lennie found a job and went their separate ways.
How was he involved in the case?
Chuck states that he was “very close” to the both of them. Especially Lennie Small. Chuck Long said he new them the best out of everyone. He explained that he was not surprised that Lennie had died. As sad as it was, he said that George could be very controlling at times and George would be the only one crazy enough to kill someone. When asked who he thought killed Lennie Small, he responded with,“I think George done it, I think he done it alright. I saw that river inconet and I think George would be the only one who would take that extra step.”
