Having Fun for No Money

By Ian
Though times are tough during the great depression there are still many ways to have a good time in the Salinas valley. People don’t have the money to go and buy their food, games and they don’t have to pay to go to a concert. For many the solution can be simple- do it with yourself and your neighbors. Also, getting some people to play some football is a great idea. If your local church puts on a social event then that’s a great opportunity to have some for no money at all. Having a small concert with your musical neighbors could be quite fun, as is listening to music while you talk and play with your friends, or if you have a deck of cards you can lay a ton of different games that will keep you entertained for hours. If it’s a nice day out then you might want to go play football with your friends. If you’re bored when you leave school, then you could go to one of the programs they offer and keep you busy for a while, but if not you could go to a local dance and you could meet someone new and have a great time. While it’s still light you can go to a rodeo and be around your friends. Once it’s dark and you can’t play outside anymore then the parents can listen to the radio or read the newspaper while the kids read some comics. Though the money is tight doesn’t mean you can’t have fun, there are so many fun things to do without much money. By making your own entertainment you can never run out of things to do and it all costs nothing.
