The New Deal

Will this truely save us?

By Greg
What shall we do next? Our economy is at an all time low, jobs are lost, even families homeless! Luckily, there is hope. Our president, Mr. Roosevelt, has created the New Deal. The New Deal is a series of federal programs, work projects, finance reforms, and regulations to help boost our economy and free us all from this catastrophe. On March 4th, 1933, the New Deal was put in place. It created the Public Works Administration. This program will give us all jobs along with roads, buildings, and other structures to improve our infrastructure. It’s a win win situation. The New Deal also inaugurated the Works Progress Administration in April of 1935. Whether you are a skilled professional, an artist, or a cook, you could sign up, get a job, and get paid. This will benefit us all which again, will boost the economy. Alongside all of this, the president established the Tennessee Valley Authority. It is able to produce and provide cheap electricity for farmers in a seven state area, where only a fraction of the inhabitants have access to electricity to operate their machines and to light their homes. To conclude, this New Deal will truely save us all from this economic crisis. No matter your expertise, race, or gender you can get a job and support yourself and your family. What are you waiting for?
