George’s Boss Testifies Against Him

By Ian J

Today John Tyler testified in court on the behalf of the prosecution at 9am on thursday. John Tyler is the owner and boss at the ranch Lennie and George worked at. He owned the ranch since he was 17, and Curley is his only living family member. Mr. Tyler claims that in the short interaction he had with them that George was controlling of Lennie and very inconsiderate, he claims that he mistreated his son’s wife and Lennie. When Mr. Tyler was cross examined by the defense he was asked if the short interaction was enough to justify George killing Lennie in cold blood. The defence was sure to get exactly what they wanted with simple yes or no answers but took it a little to far. He tried to convey that George didn’t really like Lennie and wasn’t really his friend.
