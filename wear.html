1930s fashion: What did people wear?

By Alana

Women and men would dress up nicely during the 1930’s, despite the Great Depression. Clothing and attire would be like an escape from reality, to try to calm everyone during this stressful time. Common attire for men consisted of sporting fedora and double breasted overcoats. Similarly, boys would also dress nicely with a combination of shorts and tall socks. Women would tend to wear dresses with floral patterns which were very popular at the time. Fashion was glamorous despite that money was scarce. Women would also wear mascara, pencil-in their eyebrows and would put on eyeshadow to look their very best. With how most people dressed at the time, it’s hard to even know how the economy was doing.
