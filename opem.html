Defense Opening Statement

By Narine

Today at 8:55 in the courtroom, a defense lawyer presented the opening statement for the defense team. They read their opening statement, which included why George
(Milton) is wrongfully accused of the 1st degree murder of Lennie Smalls. They explained the brother-like relationship between George and Lennie and how George always looked out for Lennie and how that proves that he is not guilty because he had no reason to kill him. They also stated that George had no motive to kill, and was only trying to “defend the safety of others”. Lastly, the defense team made it clear to the jury that it George “Did not kill with malicious intent” which makes him not guilty of the account of 1st degree murder.
