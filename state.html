Prosecution Lawyer Opening Statement

It’s the first day of the trial. All the people in the court are hectic. The judge hit the gavel on the table. After going through the usual ceremonies, such as saying their names and role, pledging to only say the truth, the prosecution lawyer stood up, and the judge asked whether or not they thought that George Milton was guilty. The short answer “guilty” started the opening statement by the lawyer. “Mr. Milton treated Mr. Small with kindness. They had an unbreakable friendship, and Mr. Milton killed Mr. Small to free him of pain, but that doesn't justify his actions. When George left the ranch, he had a gun with him, proving that he has planned the murder. We think George Milton commited murder in the 1st degree.” As said by the prosecution lawyer. After finishing the speech, the lawyer sat down and the questioning of witnesses began.
