Chuck Long At The Stand of the George Milton Trial

By Emma
\
Chuck Long has been in migrant workers since he was fourteen years old. He is thirty two years old currently. Unfortunately he lost his dad when he was twenty six
years old. He went to California and San Francisco with George and Lennie. As Chuck Long was traveling with George and Lennie, He realized that George and Lennie had a huge difference in power. Mr. Long said that George took advantage of Lennie and made fun of him. Long said that George threw rocks at Lennie’s head and pushed him into a river and almost let him drown. Mr. Long thought that if he wasn’t there to witness what George had done, George wouldn’t have saved Lennie. Mr. Long believes that George would be the only one to take that extra step and kill Lennie small. He felt closer to Lennie than George. Mr. Long stated that he wouldn’t be surprised if George did kill Lennie small. He says George was a bit controlling. Overall, Chuck Long does believe that George Milton was the one who kill Lennie Small.
