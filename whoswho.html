Who's Who: Curly Tyler

By Ian

Who is he?
Curley Tyler is a 24 year old who works at his dad’s ranch, keeping track of taxes. He met his wife at the riverside dance palace. He didn’t have a very good relationship with Lennie and when he found out he didn’t know what to do and was in shock and went after Lennie in a storm of anger.
How was he involved in the case?
Curley’s wife was killed hours before Lennie’s murder. Curley after seeing his dead wife went after Lennie with a group of men from his father’s ranch. After searching, the men come upon a small grove seeing Lennie dead with a bullet hole in the back of his head. George was sitting next to him with Carlson’s Luger beside George.
