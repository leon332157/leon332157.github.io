Second Day

By Cara and Keelin

Today on the second day of the trial, Josiah Lincoln took the stand to be questioned. This took place at about 9:00 AM. Josiah Lincoln is a long time worker on the farm, but didn't really talk to
anyone accept Slim Johnson, who’s another witness on the trail. But, for the short period of time that Mr.Milton and Mr.Small were on the ranch, Josiah saw them to be very close like brothers, this was very unusual at the time for two people to travel together. George took care of Lennie, and Josiah didn't think that he used him to get jobs. On the day of the incident Mr.Small wandered into Josiah's barn and talked to him about the “dream” that Mr.Milton and him had shared. The “dream” is something that they had been planning for a while, they were going to buy some land and live on it in total freedom. After the incident had happened with Jane Tyler and Lennie Small, the guys on the Tyler Ranch wanted to kill Lennie. Mr.Milton didn't know what to do , so he killed Lennie because he cared for him and wanted Len n ie’s death to be by someone he was close to . He wanted him to have a quick and painless death in the hands of someone who cared for him, rather than all the guys looking to kill him. Josiah did not believe that Mr.Milton committed the murder.
