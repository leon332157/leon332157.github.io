Defense Closing statement

Today, the defense stated their final plead for George’s innocence. They said that George killed his friend, Lennie Small, to avoid the torture from Curley Tyler.
They also said that because Lennie couldn’t control his strength, it was better that he dies rather than him constantly harming others in his path. They also said that all the prosecution’s `witnesses were biased and that they were unreliable sources because they barely knew George and Lennie. Lastly, they asked if you can truly ruin a man’s life for doing what he felt what was best.
