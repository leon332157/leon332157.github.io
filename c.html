Slim’s Testimony

By Marianna

Day 2 of the George Milton trial, and Curley Tyler is called to the stand. Curley is a 24 year old man and is the son of the owner of the ranch where the murder occurred. Curley met
George and Lennie on the day that they arrived at the ranch. Curley stated that he asked Lennie a question, but Lennie didn’t answer the question until George told him that he could. This already showed that George had power over Lennie. At one point, George told Lennie to hurt Curley, and Lennie listened to George, this ended up with Curley having many crushed bones in his hand. Lennie was unaware of the strength that he had, which also led to Lennie killing Curley's wife, Jane. Curley said Lennie might be a danger to others because he is so unaware of the strength he has. When the other men including Curley and George found out that Lennie killed Jane, they all had different emotions. Curley first had hope that his wife was still alive but was then shocked and angry to find out that Lennie had killed her. Curley states that George got angry and then disappeared from the group. Later, all the men found Lennie on the ground with blood coming out of the back of his head and George with a gun in his hand about 10 feet behind him. So overall, Curley believes and showed that George murdered Lennie.
