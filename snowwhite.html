Snow White And The Seven Dwarfs- Disney’s Folly?

By Shaqueh

Walt Disney’s first full length feature production, Snow White And The Seven Dwarfs, is one of the first full length
animation productions. There are a lot of contradictory thoughts regarding the subject. Some people say “Who’s gonna pay to see a motion picture about dwarfs- let alone animated!”. Even Disney himself has been doubting that his movie would succeed. He tried perfecting it to the maximum of his extent. And all of it was worth it. After hours and hours of sleepless nights, the picture was created and sent to cinema’s all over USA. Despite the movie receiving early hate and critique, everyone was on the end of their seats while watching it. So was it a hit? Some say "times make the man" and some say "man makes the times." In the case of Walt Disney, it was definitely the latter. The movie succeeded, even though the creator himself thought that it would be a flop.



The movie grew up to $2 million! No other movie has made that amount of money in the recent 10 years. Is this the beginning of a big animation studio, or is this just a one time hit? Only time will tell.
