Who's Who: Marie Jones

By Maddie

Who is she?
Marie Jones, also known as the woman in the red dress is 18 years old and was born and raised in Weed, a town North of the Tyler Ranch. She does field work on the farm Lennie Small and George Milton worked on when they lived in Weed. She has never been to the Tyler Ranch.
How was she involved in the case?
One day when Marie Jones was walking in town wearing a red dress, Lennie Small came up to her. This would have been fine if he didn’t keep insisting on how pretty it was and how much he wanted to touch it. Now that alone is enough to be a bit frightening for a young lady such as Marie but it didn’t stop there. He then started to pet her dress and when she tried to tell him to stop and get away from her, Lennie suddenly grabbed onto the dress and wouldn’t let go. He didn’t release her until George Milton whacked Lennie over the head. This was a traumatizing event for Marie who’s friends urged her to report it to the authorities immediately. When the officers heard about the incident they went out to find Lennie Small but were unable to find him as he had run away with George Milton. Marie Jones hadn’t previously interacted with George or Lennie besides the occasional appearance on the farm they all worked on. Her encounter with Lennie Small was the reason he had to flee South to the Tyler Ranch. After the day of her attack she didn’t see George Milton or Lennie Small.
