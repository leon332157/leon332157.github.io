Marie Jones

By Maddie
On the first day of the George Milton trial, Marie Jones testified on her encounter with Mr. Lennie Small. She is eighteen years old and lives in Weed. She only spoke
once with Mr. Small, when he attacked her. She claims Mr. Small came up to her, by himself, as she didn’t see Mr. Milton, he proceeded to aggressively grab her and her dress. Ms. Jones’s mother was murdered by her father after they fought when she was a child. Since then she has been wary of powerful, aggressive men like Mr. Small. After he let go of her and her dress, Ms. Jones went to the police and in a panic reported that she was raped by Mr. Small. She stated that if she told the whole truth or dismissed it as something less than she did that she wouldn’t have been taken seriously by authorities. Ms. Jones wasn’t harmed by Mr. Milton and she didn’t know them closely, only observing them from a distance. While working in the fields at the ranch that Mr. Milton and Mr. Small she would sometimes see them and when she did she could tell that Mr. Milton had a considerable amount of control of Mr. Small.
